We are building an ecommerce in agriculture system where farmers can upload products and customer can buy and connect with farmers directly. 

6.2 Incremental Implementation Plan

Our system will be developed in following increments.
6.2.1 Increment 1: Foundation & Farmer Portal
•	Environment & Architecture
– Initialize the Django project with sqlite and React app.
– Define the database schema for Users (with farmer/dealer roles).
•	Authentication & Profiles
– Implement sign-up, login, and profile management for both farmers and dealers.
•	Farmer “Sell” Section
–Build CRUD interface for Farmers to create, read, update, and delete product listings (name, description, price, quantity, images).
– Design Farmer’s own dashboard showing their active listings.

6.2.2 Increment 2: Dealer/Customer Experience & Location Features
•	Dealer/Customer “Buy” Section
–Implement product catalog browsing, detail pages, and filtering/search (keyword, category, price range).
– Enable Dealers to add items to a cart or purchase list (no payment integration).
•	Geolocation & Mapping
– Integrate Google Maps API (or similar) for geolocation.
– Build section to display nearest farmers or users.

6.2.3 Increment 3: Admin, Testing and Documentation
•	Admin Inventory Management
– Configure Django’s admin panel for CRUD on farming tools, machinery, and crop categories.
– Expose a public “Inventory” page for users to browse admin-managed items.
